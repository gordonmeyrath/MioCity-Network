## ğŸ”„ Workflows

### **Click & Collect Workflow**

1. Customer browses products in mobile app
2. Customer adds items to cart and selects pickup time
3. Customer completes order and receives QR code
4. Business receives order notification
5. Business prepares order and marks as ready
6. Customer receives ready notification
7. Customer arrives and shows QR code
8. Business scans QR code to verify and complete pickup
9. Customer earns loyalty stamps/points

### **Issue Reporting Workflow**

1. Citizen discovers local issue (broken streetlight, pothole, etc.)
2. Citizen opens app and selects issue category
3. Citizen takes photos and confirms GPS location
4. Citizen adds description and submits report
5. Municipality receives notification
6. Municipality assigns issue to appropriate department
7. Municipality updates status as work progresses
8. Citizen receives status update notifications
9. Municipality marks issue as resolved
10. Citizen confirms resolution

### **Restaurant Reservation Workflow**

1. Customer browses local restaurants
2. Customer views menu and selects date/time
3. Customer specifies party size and special requests
4. Customer receives confirmation with QR code
5. Restaurant receives reservation notification
6. Restaurant prepares for arrival
7. Customer arrives and shows QR code
8. Restaurant scans code and seats party
9. Customer earns loyalty rewards
